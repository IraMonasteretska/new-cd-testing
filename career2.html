The Care Indeed Company

They had a dream to transform the way home care was delivered, by focusing on the employees. Care Indeed is the first caregiver staffing agency to use innovative technology and programs to train its team. We have four offices and over 700 employees; for the past three years, we have ranked in the Top 50 Best Companies to Work For in caregiving jobs in San Francisco.

Working for Care Indeed

Care Indeed provides a range of benefits to our team, including:

  • Benefits packages, including 401K matching
  • Competitive pay ($18-$20/hour)
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Per diem options
  • Paid mileage
  • Exclusive discounts at partnering companies
  • Extensive training and support
  • Opportunities for upward mobility

To work as a caregiver for Care Indeed, you must:

  • Enjoy working with senior citizens
  • Have Hoyer Lift experience
  • Own a smartphone
  • Pass a background check
  • Submit a current TB test
  • Hold a valid driver’s license
  • Be authorized to work in the US
  • Stay up-to-date with COVID-19 vaccinations

As part of our San Francisco caregiver staffing team, you will:

  • Follow the agency care plan when providing personal care services
  • Follow all company policies and procedures
  • Assist with daily living activities
  • Help with personal care and hygiene
  • Provide mobility support
  • Complete light housekeeping tasks
  • Run errands and transport clients
  • Prepare meals
  • Offer companionship
  • Execute other responsibilities as needed
