FAQs

What are your reschedule, cancellation, and refund policies?

We will accommodate rescheduling or canceling of shifts if we are notified 24 hours prior. Refunds will only be considered for documented medical emergencies.

Can the Standard or Premium Packages be customized with additional services?

No, however, we offer a Custom Package which allows for full customization to accommodate clients who are looking for flexible options. Clients can tailor the volume and type of care to match their needs.

Is Couple’s Care eligible for these bundles?

Yes. We can tailor Care Bundle to accommodate Couple’s Care, such as placing a caregiver who can assist with both clients’ care needs.

Will long-term insurance cover Care Bundle?

Yes. However, each insurance is different. We highly recommend that you check with your insurance carriers. As long as there are 6 ALS Activities of Daily Living that will be performed by the Home Care Aide (HCA), it’s covered.

How are Care Bundle paid?

Clients on all plans will be invoiced weekly. Clients can settle their payments using their private bank accounts or via insurance.
