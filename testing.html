Client COVID Testing FAQs

Is the test available to everyone?

Yes, the test is available to everyone.

What test is available?

We provide PCR and Same-Day Antigen test.

Does the service require insurance?

No, the service doesn’t require insurance, however there will be an extra charge for PCR test if you don’t have an insurance.

What service areas do we cover?

We provide services in the following counties: San Mateo County, San Francisco County, and Santa Clara County.

What is the cost of the COVID Testing Service?

The cost of COVID-19 testing varies. Costs range from PCR $99-149 with 24-48 hours processing time. Same-Day Antigen costs $69.

Is Care Indeed testing able to meet the travel or entry requirements to any destination?

Yes, we are in accordance with CDC’s guidelines for travel. Test specimens are processed in a reputable laboratory, and results are generated electronically.

What type of testing method is required for other countries?

Please refer to COVID-19 Travel Recommendations by Destination here.

What is the cancellation and rechedule policy

All cancellations or recheduleing requires a 24-hour notice in advance. A cancelation fee (full amount) will be withheld from any refund.

How it works?

Schedule a visit by requesting an appointment online. Our nurses will go to your home to perform the COVID testing you selected. You will receive your test results within 24h to 48h.

How do I get the results?

Your test results will be delivered to your registered email. If you have not received the result within the timeframe or have any questions, please call (650) 461-4554 or send an email to covidsupport@careindeed.com.
